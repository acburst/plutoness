YIN PROPAGANDA

My main work sample is “Yin Propaganda” - a commercial for an idea. This video piece weaves together interests in dreamwork as analog for memory and story, poetry as political speech, Chinese philosophy, and the weaponization of the nondominant via aesthetics. Part educational PSA-turned hypnotic mind control, part televised mystery theater, the piece explores the power of TV to control public discourse by operating in the private-collective space. “Yin Propaganda” demonstrates interest in Chinese Philosophy and identity, improvisation, as well as an interest in sound art as score and visual synthesis, which I would love to continue to explore with my project if I had the means.
