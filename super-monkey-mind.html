SUPER MONKEY MIND

“Super Monkey Mind” is an experimental ambient album by Die Artist that was released by Topos Press in 2022. Die Artist is an improvisational electronic duo that I am one-half of. The piece is accompanied by a video-as-visualizer that I cut to repeat, as an exercise of deteriorating loops and the shifting meaning of image within the context of sound and music. This piece is relevant to my interest in scoring and what I can achieve with music and sound with basic audio skills. It is interested in appropriation of cultural material via sampling. The footage features a lone Golden Buddha statue in the middle of the Mojave Desert, seemingly a site of grassroots pilgrimage and icon worship. The video attempts to reorient the Buddha within a magical framework of interacting with the universe, touching on Asian Religion and the process it underwent of de-mysticism and intellectualization as it was appropriated into Western paradigm. It also explores the way that spirituality occupies the private-collective space.
