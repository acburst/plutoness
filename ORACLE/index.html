ORACLE /ˈôrək(ə)l/: a clear glass straw collapsing past, present and future;
a site of decolonization of TIME.

A node of our evolutionary divergence from other primates is identified in the moment human cognition developed the ability for PROSPECTION, or, MENTAL TIME-TRAVEL. We simulate, we plan, we predict, we intend. Foresight is key to the particular way we make decisions.

STILL WE ARE HAUNTED by a lack of agency. It is humanity's embarrassment, Achille's Heel- our inability to see around the next corner. A sense of control reincarnates in the form of superstition: an imagined link between certain actions or psychic phenomena and specific effects in the external world. Collectively we pour a vast amount of energy into knowing the unknowable. In certain areas we seem to actually have some sort of edge. We huddle behind the lanterns of our world class fortune-telling experts. But prediction is ultimately a strenuous, difficult, and largely innaccurate business.
